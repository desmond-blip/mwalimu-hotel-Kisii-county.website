Types of Dining

Fine Dining

Elegant atmosphere, high-quality food, and professional service. Often includes multiple courses and dress codes.

Casual Dining

Relaxed environment with table service and moderately priced menus. Ideal for families or casual outings.

Fast Food

Quick service, inexpensive meals, often takeaway-focused. Includes burgers, fries, pizza, etc.
